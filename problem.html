Problem

We are addressing the ongoing farmers' protest in Haryana and Punjab, primarily due to unfulfilled government promises from the 2020-21 protest. The farmers demand a legal guarantee on Minimum Support Price (MSP) for crops, pensions for farmers and farm laborers, a farm debt waiver, and no increase in electricity tariffs.

Our founder and co-founder have diligently researched and formulated solutions that will soon be presented to the parliament
